    • Plot Overview

    2024's Damsel directed by Juan Carlos Fresnadillo features Millie Bobby Brown as Elodie trapped in a dark fantasy world.
    In this intriguing tale, "Millie Bobby Brown, known for her role in “Stranger Things”", defies the traditional damsel-in-distress trope.
    Heres a summary of the film:
    Setting the Stage: The story begins with a glimpse of the past, where a king and his army venture into a mountain cave in search of treasure.
    However, they encounter a fire-breathing dragon, resulting in the soldiers demise. Only the king survives, fate unknown.
    Present Day: Several centuries later, we find an impoverished kingdom struggling to survive due to resource scarcity.
    The ruler, King Bayford(played by Ray Winstone), faces challenges in providing for his subjects.
    Princess Elodie(played by Millie Bobby) and her sister Floria(played by Brooke Carter) lead humble lives,
    sharing chores and gathering resources like everyone else.
    The Unexpected Proposal: A fancy carriage arrives, surprising everyone. It carries a marriage proposal for Elodie from the distant kingdom
    of Aurea. Prince Henry(played by Nick Robinson), Elodies prospective husband, possesses vast wealth and a fleet of ships.
    Despite Elodies desire to explore the world independently, she agrees to the marriage for the sake of her people.
    The Twist: Elodie discovers that her marriage was a trap. The royal family intends to sacrifice her to repay an ancient debt.
    She finds herself in a cave with a fire-breathing dragon, relying solely on her wits and determination to survive.
    No Knight in Shining Armor: Unlike traditional fairy tales, there is no knight waiting to rescue Elodie.
    She must navigate danger, uncover lies, and forge her own path to safety.
    While “Damsel” doesnt break new ground, its visual effects and overall plot make for an enjoyable watch.
    Elodies journey from princess to survivor is both captivating and empowering.

    Overview & Characters

    The first king of Aurea attacks a dragon but fails, leaving his men dead. Centuries later, Elodie agrees to marry Prince Henry to help her people.
    At their wedding, they participate in a ritual where Elodie learns about the first king's sacrifice.
    Henry betrays her by throwing her into the dragon's lair, but she survives and finds a way out.
    She collects glowing slugs for light and discovers a safe chamber with a map of past victims.
    As she rests, the slugs heal her burn. Elodie follows a map to a dead end on a mountain with remains of dead dragon hatchlings, revealing royal sacrifices.
    Lord Bayford's rescue party is killed by the dragon, allowing Elodie to escape. Isabelle kidnaps Elodie's sister as a replacement sacrifice.
    Elodie distracts the dragon to rescue Floria, convincing the dragon they were deceived by Aureans. Elodie defeats the dragon and exposes Aureans' treachery at a wedding,
    leading to their demise. Elodie, Floria, and Lady Bayford return home with the dragon.

    Release

    Damsel was scheduled to stream on Netflix on October 13, 2023.
    However, due to the impact of the 2023 SAG-AFTRA strike on the film's promotion,
    the original release date was postponed and the film began streaming on March 8, 2024.

    Novel

    Author Evelyn Skye released a novelization of the film in advance. She stated:
    "The easiest way to think about it is this: Dan Mazeau wrote the original screenplay.
    I was able to read an early draft and was given free rein to write my own version of the story, which ultimately became the novel.
    Both the novel and the movie may stem from the same origin, but they are also each their own unique works of art".
