Damsel

Written by Dan Mazeau

Plot

The first king of Aurea fails to defeat a dragon in his realm, leading to a futile attack in which all his men are killed.
Centuries later, Elodie accepts a marriage proposal from Prince Henry to help her people, even though the queen disapproves.
After a treacherous wedding ritual, Elodie discovers the truth behind the dragon sacrifices.
She escapes the dragon's lair with the help of glowing slugs and rescues her sister by outsmarting the dragon.
Elodie exposes the Aureans' deception and escapes with her family, accompanied by the dragon.
